Instructor Dashboard – Patient Safety Logic

Item #42 – Instructor Analysis

Clinical Scenario

Patient becomes short of breath while changing position in bed.

Correct Action

Stop activity and assess breathing

Clinical Rationale (Medical Situation)

Shortness of breath during movement may indicate acute respiratory compromise, hypoxia, or cardiovascular instability. Immediate assessment is required to prevent deterioration. Continuing activity or delaying assessment increases risk of patient harm.

Distractor Analysis

Option Candidate Thinking Unsafe Reason Prometric Signal
Encourage deep breathing later “It will pass” / minimize symptom Ignore ABC Fails airway/breathing priority
Document Task completion mindset Delay action Paperwork before patient safety
Notify physician Authority dependency Wait for order Nurse did not act within scope

Prometric Target (What is Being Tested)

  • Recognition of early respiratory compromise
  • Immediate nurse-led intervention without delay
  • ABC prioritization over hierarchy or documentation

Instructor Action Guidance

  • If missed → mark as Unsafe Reasoning
  • 2+ similar misses → flag candidate for remediation
  • Pattern across cohort → instructor calibration required
